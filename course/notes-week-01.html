    Section Week 1: January 12

    This week begins with an exploration of systems of linear equations and the possibilities for sets of solutions. Then we will develop an algorithm that transforms a system of equations into a form, called reduced row echelon form, that enables us to quickly draw conclusions about its set of solutions.
